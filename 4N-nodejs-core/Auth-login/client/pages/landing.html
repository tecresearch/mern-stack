    About Flyte

    Flyte is revolutionizing urban transportation with our cutting-edge autonomous helicopter service. Designed for the modern commuter, we provide a fast, efficient, and luxurious alternative to ground transportation in congested metropolitan areas.

    Our mission is to reduce urban congestion and carbon emissions while providing an unparalleled travel experience. With Flyte, your commute becomes an opportunity to relax, work, or simply enjoy breathtaking city views.

    Founded in 2023 by aerospace engineers and urban mobility experts, Flyte combines advanced aviation technology with smart city integration to create a seamless transportation network above your city.

    Our Technology

    Autonomous Flight

    Our helicopters feature state-of-the-art autonomous navigation systems with multiple redundancy layers for maximum safety. Powered by AI, they can adapt to changing weather conditions and air traffic in real-time.

    Eco-Friendly Power

    Flyte's hybrid-electric propulsion system delivers zero-emission flights with whisper-quiet operation. Our energy-efficient design allows for quick charging and extended range between charges.

    Smart App Integration

    The Flyte app provides real-time booking, flight tracking, and personalized preferences. Our intuitive interface makes urban air travel as simple as hailing a ride.
